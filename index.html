Project Showcase

Project 1: Comprehensive LMS Migration & Digital Learning Transformation

Challenge: The organization relied on traditional, in-person training and disparate physical resources, leading to inconsistencies, difficulty in tracking progress, and high administrative overhead. A centralized, digital solution was needed to scale training.

My Role: As LMS Administrator/Technical Support Specialist, I analyzed content, designed the LMS information architecture, converted materials into SCORM-compliant modules, managed users, generated reports, and provided technical support.

Approach & Tools:

ADDIE Model Articulate Storyline 360 Articulate Rise Adobe Captivate Cornerstone LMS

Impact: +25% User Engagement

Visualizing the growth in user engagement and comprehension after digital transformation.

Project 2: Agile-Driven Onboarding Application Development

Challenge: The customer feedback process was inefficient, causing delayed responses and potential frustration during critical early interactions. A more streamlined system was needed to capture and act on feedback quickly.

My Role: As a member of the ServiceNow NextGen Program, I contributed to conceptualizing and configuring custom applications, using form configuration and scripting. I actively participated in Agile sprints, project planning, and cross-functional collaboration.

Approach & Tools:

Agile Framework ServiceNow Form Configuration Scripting SME Coordination

Impact & Results

  • Improved Customer Satisfaction: The application decreased feedback response time, boosting satisfaction ratings and supporting a smoother customer onboarding experience.
  • Enhanced Platform Functionality: Demonstrated ability to configure and script within a complex system to meet specific business needs and improve operational efficiency.

Project 3: Technical Documentation & Training Program for Software Adoption

Challenge: Users frequently encountered issues with specialized applications, leading to high support ticket volumes and reduced productivity. Clear, effective, and accessible training and reference materials were needed.

My Role: In technical support roles, I identified user pain points, developed step-by-step technical documentation (guides, FAQs), designed and delivered engaging training sessions, maintained a documentation repository, and analyzed incident data for improvements.

Approach & Tools:

Needs Assessment SharePoint Remedy Ticketing WebEx Self-Service Docs

Impact: High Performance Metrics

Achieved high rates in both customer satisfaction and technical issue resolution through proactive training and documentation.
